Health Companion Super App - UX Case Study

A UX/UI Design project to assist individuals with chronic health conditions.

Introduction

The Health Companion Super App is designed to help individuals manage their medications and doctor appointments efficiently. This project focuses on creating a user-friendly experience that addresses key pain points identified through user research.

User Research

Through extensive research, we identified the following major challenges faced by individuals with chronic health conditions:

  • Medication Adherence Issues: Forgetfulness, complex regimens, and side effects.
  • Appointment Management Difficulties: Anxiety, scheduling conflicts, and communication barriers.
  • Technology & Accessibility Barriers: Poor usability in existing health apps, difficulty navigating interfaces.

User Personas

Emily Carter (25, Freelancer)
