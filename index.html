Introduction

HealthX is a mobile-first application designed to streamline medication tracking and appointment scheduling. Our goal is to create an intuitive and accessible platform for users managing chronic health conditions.

User Research

Through extensive research, we identified key pain points affecting users:

  • Medication Adherence Issues: 82% of users forget to take their medication on time.
  • Appointment Scheduling Problems: 74% struggle with booking and managing appointments.
  • Accessibility Concerns: Older users need larger fonts and voice-based reminders.

Survey & Interview Questions

To understand user needs, we conducted surveys and interviews. Sample questions included:

  • How often do you forget to take your medication?
  • Would you prefer AI-driven reminders or manual notifications?
  • What features would help improve your medication adherence?
